About

Trending Instant-Check will provide social media users with a one-stop tool to consume information while being able to instantly cross-check any suspicious content they come across.
The platform will not only collect newsworthy information from the major social media platforms across a range of topics (politics, health, science) and variety of media formats (text, videos, images and memes), but also employ AI and Natural Language Processing to provide users with additional context and related fact-checks alongside the consumed information:
  • If there is a fact-check story already done for a piece of content, the fact-check will automatically be presented alongside the content, highlighted in red, to give users further context.
  • If users come across suspicious content that has not been checked yet, they could instantly click the search icon and submit the content to be checked, directly from this platform to the fact-checking organization.
The platform introduces a new business model for fact-checking content - personalized native advertising. This will not only enrich the recommendations with journalistic content from trustworthy publishers, but also contribute to the sustainability of this project.
